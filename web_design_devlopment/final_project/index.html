Welcome to RARE BOOKS Inc. We specialize in rare, collectible and high-value books. We were established in 1987. Click on Browse from the menu to see some selections from our catalogue.

Do you have books for sale? We are always buying. Please call the store or send us an email using the 'E-Mail Us' link in the menu to make arrangments for one of our professional staff to evaluate your collection. We pay cash for all purchases worth $100.00 or less otherwise we will issue a cheque to whomever you request is the payee.

Do you need insurance on your collection? Has it been a while since you had your collection appraised? We have all the necessary expertise to appraise any collection of rare books. Please email us or call to setup an appointment with one of our specialists. We will come to your library to limit handling of your items however please be aware there is a small call-out fee associated with these types of appointments.

Appraisal Service Fees:

  • Call-Out: $40.00
  • Appraisal (per hour): $75.00/hour
  • Minimum cost for collections (30 minutes): $37.50
  • Single book appraisal (in-store): $1.00 - $10.00
  • Consultation: varies

To see our current appointment openings click on the 'Appointments' option in the menu. This has up-to-date information on our specialists' availability to better serve you.

Can't make it down to the store but still have books for sale? Click the 'Sell to Us' option and fill out the form. We will review your request and get back to you with an offer! We will require photos of the book submitted and may request additional images.

Want to know more? Have a read of our About Us page which tells our whole story.

Do you have a passion for rare books? Do you love our store? We might be looking for someone like you. Come back often and review our Careers section. All of our career opportunities are posted publicly here and it's a great place to work! Working with us is not just a job, it's a career.
